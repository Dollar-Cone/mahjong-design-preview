About

A quiet room, a good table, and people who are glad you came.

We're a small Mahjong studio in Mineral Springs, North Carolina — just south of Charlotte. Good light, comfortable chairs, real tiles, and tea always on. We started because the people who love this game deserved a real room to play it in.

Curious whether the studio is for you? Stop by, or send us a note →

Your first visit, plainly.

Most first-time visitors come in for a class or to watch open play. Here's what to expect, so you can walk in knowing what happens next.

1

You arrive.

Park out front or in the side lot — both are free. Walk in. We'll see you and come say hi. No check-in counter, no clipboard. You don't have to know anyone.

2

We point you at the right table.

New to mahjong? You'll sit with a teacher or at an assisted-play table. Played before? We'll put you with three other players around your level. You don't have to know what level you are — we'll figure it out together.

3

You play.

A few hands. Tea or coffee. Conversation. Stay an hour or stay the afternoon. There's no minimum and no pressure to perform — the people at the table know what it's like to be new to the room.

4

You come back, if you want.

We don't chase. If you liked it, you'll be back. If you want a class, we have one. If you want to make this a habit, ask about membership. If you want to think about it for a week, think about it for a week.
