Help

Questions, answered plainly.

The most common ones first. If your question isn't here, the studio is happy to answer it directly — usually within a business day.

Ask the studio →

If you're new to the studio.

I've never played mahjong. Where do I start?→ A Mahjong 101 class is the friendliest first step — two hours, no experience needed. Can I just come and watch?→ Yes. Stop by during open play, look around, ask anything. No appointment needed. Is the studio only for experienced players?→ Not at all. Half the people in the room on any given day learned in the last year. How much does it cost to play?→ Open play is $12 for drop-in, free for members. Classes start at $30. What should I bring my first time?→ Just yourself. Tiles, tea, and pencils are on us. Do you teach the American style? National Mah Jongg League?→ Yes — that's the version most members play. Other styles can be arranged on request.

Visiting the studio.

Where are you, and where do I park?→ 5910 Waxhaw Hwy, Mineral Springs. Free parking out front and on the side lot. When are you open?→ Tuesday through Saturday, 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Closed Sunday and Monday. Is the studio accessible?→ Yes — single floor, no steps at the entrance, accessible restrooms. Can I bring a friend who doesn't play?→ Of course. We have a comfortable lounge area with tea, books, and good light.

Booking, memberships, and the rest.

How do I book a class or a session?→ Find what you want, pick a date, and pay. Or call the studio — we'll do it for you. How do I cancel or reschedule?→ Sign in and adjust your booking, or call us — free up to 24 hours before. What's included in membership?→ See the membership page for the plain list. Can I host a private group at the studio?→ Yes — birthdays, work outings, charity nights. Tell us what you're planning.
