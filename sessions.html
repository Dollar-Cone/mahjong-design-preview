What kind of session is right for you?

OPEN PLAY

Just come and play

You sit down, we make sure you have three other players. The most common way to spend an afternoon here.

ASSISTED

With a teacher in the room

Same idea, but a teacher is nearby to answer questions and gently steer when you're stuck.

MEMBER NIGHT

Smaller, quieter, no reservations needed. The same faces, the better you know each other.
