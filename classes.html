How learning works at the studio.

Three steps, no hurry. Most members find their footing in two or three weeks.

STEP ONE

Take Mahjong 101

A friendly two-hour intro. We teach the basics with real tiles, real conversation, and no pressure.

STEP TWO

Practice in open play

Come back for an assisted-play session and use what you learned. A teacher is in the room.

STEP THREE

Join a level-two class

Strategy, scoring, and faster hands — when you're ready. There's no schedule but yours.

ALWAYS

Ask, anytime

If a class is full or you'd like a private lesson, the studio is happy to set something up.
